Security Protocol

End-to-End Encryption

Your privacy is our priority. All medical chats and video consultations are fully encrypted. No third party, not even Homigo Care, can access your private conversations.

Account & Login Safety

We use a robust One-Time Password (OTP) system for verification. Your passwords are never stored in plain text; we use advanced cryptographic hashing to keep your credentials safe.

Secured Data Storage

All medical records and personal identification documents are stored in a secured Firebase Realtime Database with restricted server-side rules to prevent unauthorized access.

Anti-Fraud Monitoring

Our systems actively monitor for suspicious activities and fake doctor profiles. We perform rigorous verification on every medical expert registered on our platform.

Security Report

Found a vulnerability or a bug? Report it directly to our security lead.
